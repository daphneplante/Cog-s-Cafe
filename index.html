Mission Statement

Cog’s Café is a small town coffeehouse and bakery located in Hardwick, Vermont. This family owned café seeks to inspire and nurture the human spirit – one person, one cup and one neighborhood at a time. To use the ingredients provided locally as much as possible and be transparent about where our ingredients come from. To ensure that each guest receives prompt, professional, friendly and courteous service. To maintain a clean, comfortable and well maintained premises for our guests and staff. To thank each guest for the opportunity to serve them. By maintaining these objectives we shall be assured of a fair profit that will allow us to contribute to the community we serve.

Where are we?

Cog's Café is located in Hardwick, Vermont. A once small farming town in Chittenden County bursting with the potential for bigger and better things.
